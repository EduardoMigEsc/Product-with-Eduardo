Hello, I am Eduardo!

I am a Product Manager with 10+ years of generalist Product experience in startups and scale-ups in Europe.

I started my Product career in London after getting a MsC in Finance and a Master in Business Administration . I gained my first practical experience in the Tech-world in two London startups before moving to Madrid to launch a French startup in Spain. Since then, it has been my mission to bridge the gap between company objectives and their product vision. Over the last 10 years, I have helped Tech companies, some unicorns, some corporations, develop better products. My experience spans every aspect of a Productâ€™s life cycle in industries as varied as Affiliate Marketing, FinTech, InsurTech, Banking, and SaaS.

I am passionate about building customer-centric product where the focus is both in discovery and delivery. Starting companies from scratch excites me, but I also aim to add established Product processes to developed structures.

Through my coaching and mentoring of others, I realized that I want to make it my mission to impact and support as many companies as possible.
